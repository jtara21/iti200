You find yourself in a forest.

How did you get there? It seems you were summoned...

The two people you see in front of you - an old man, and an elf woman - are looking back at you as if you are important. They are unfamiliar, and you should be more scared, but you have a gradual sense you should trust them, because the surrounding world outside the Kingdom of Aaridian is much more cruel.

Your choices are critical and shape your experience of the game. Through turn-based battles, you get to discover the unique powers of each of these characters as well as yourself in order to make it through the treacherous landscape of the Kingdom, where you will find both enemies and allies.

Our Goals

  • Simplicity over Complexity:

    We aim to create a game that is not complex, with a primary focus on storytelling instead of mechanics.

  • Aging and Imagination:

    We want to address the loss of imagination as people reach adulthood. Many of us remember being able to look around the room and easily see an entire world, but now it takes much more effort to visualize. Playing simple games such as this will increase our ability to reach that level of creativity and wonder.

  • Critical Thinking and Strategy:

    Our game encourages critical thinking and strategic decision-making despite its simplicity.

  • Replayability:

    We aim to tackle the issue of low replay value in open-world games by offering a higher level of replayability through simplicity and storytelling.
